Cheeseburger

Diner-styled Cheeseburger

Ingredients

  • 75/25 Ground Beef
  • Onions
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Buns

Steps

  1. Add butter in pan on medium
  2. Add diced onions with salt and pepper throughout
  3. Remove onions when browned and put in bowl for later
  4. Add butter in pan on medium-high
  5. Scoop ice cream-sized balls of ground beef into the pan
  6. Season with salt and pepper thoughout cooking
  7. Smash the balls into patties
  8. Flip when browned
  9. Butter buns and toast them in oven or similar for 5 minutes
  10. Add cheese to patties
  11. Place patties on buns once cheese is melted, top with onions, and serve
